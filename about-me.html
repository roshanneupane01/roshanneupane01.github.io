About Me

As a full-stack Software Engineer, I build innovative software solutions using front-end and back-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, SpringBoot, SQL, Git, and GitHub as my main tools. I enjoy finding logic and solving problems in creating robust software that meets the client's needs and expectations.

I graduated from the University of Texas at Arlington with a BS in Information Systems in December 2021, where I gained knowledge of front-end and back-end development technologies, such as HTML, CSS, UML, and Java. I also completed an Associate of Science degree in Information Systems from Dallas College North Lake Campus in 2020.

I'm a hard-working and goal-driven individual, constantly striving to broaden my knowledge and skills in software development. I have a strong work ethic and the ability to thrive in a team-based or individual work environment. My motivation is to create software that makes a positive impact on users and society.
